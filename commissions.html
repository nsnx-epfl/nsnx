Commissions

Discover the various commissions that shape the NSNX association.

What is a commission?

In NSNX, a commission is a dedicated group of students who take charge of a specific domain within the association. Each commission plays a key role in creating a dynamic and inclusive student environment.

General

The General Commission oversees internal coordination, ensures smooth communication between all teams, and supports the daily functioning of the association.

Events

From parties to themed nights, the Events Commission organizes fun and unforgettable moments for all NSNX members throughout the year.

Conferences

The Conferences Commission connects students with inspiring professionals through talks and panels, opening the door to new ideas and career paths.

Interviews

This commission highlights student voices and professional insights through engaging interviews shared on our platforms.

Team buildings

Focused on strengthening bonds, the Team Building Commission organizes activities that foster trust, collaboration, and friendship within the association.

Social medias

Responsible for the association’s online presence, the Social Media Commission keeps everyone informed and entertained through creative posts and stories.

Sponsoring

The Sponsoring Commission builds partnerships with companies and institutions, helping support NSNX activities through collaborations and funding.

Delegates

Delegates represent NSNX members and act as a bridge between students and the school administration, ensuring every voice is heard.

Drive

This commission ensures that all shared resources, files, and documents are well-organized and accessible to everyone in the association.

Website

The Website Commission develops and maintains NSNX’s online platform, providing clear access to information, events, and updates.

Hoodies

Bringing style and identity, the Hoodies Commission designs and manages the production of custom apparel that lets everyone wear NSNX with pride.
